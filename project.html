Not as simple as it seems

Sun-plus is not just a photovoltaic panel, but a real solar tracker. Its frame, entirely made with biodegradable materials, consists of two servomotors enabling the photovoltaic cell to move along the X and Y axes (dual axes tracking). This allows the photovoltaic panel to turn and always be in the best position to “follow” the sun.

Sun-plus solar tracker could be connected to an energy storage unit.

This is the green technology from a different view angle.
