A completely innovative technology

Sun-plus delivers a leading-edge system to capture solar energy. It is entirely innovative with respect to fixed photovoltaic panels. Being driven by double servomotors, our technology can in fact turn the panels allowing them to “follow” the sun. Hence, the solar cell is always perpendicular to it, delivering a much higher efficiency and absorbing much more energy than conventional cells.

It also turns out to be an enhanced solution as for disposal. Sun-plus is entirely built with biodegradable photovoltaic panels and all of the materials used for the frame are biodegradable too. Materials have been chosen on purpose aiming at lower carbon emissions when disposing the solar tracker.

We are proud of our efforts and dedication in honing an already highly efficient technology.
