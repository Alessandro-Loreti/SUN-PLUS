Two friends and one passion

We are two friends with different interests and passions, but sharing the same wish: turn our planet into a sustainable green world. This goal has made us close friends and has led us to develop our environmentally friendly design. We are two teenagers sharing an idea: enhancing photovoltaic panel efficiency and we have made it in the end! We have developed and built a solar tracker.

We hope our project will drive teenagers to turn their ideas into ground-breaking designs, getting in the game with courage and enthusiasm.

Alessandro Loreti and Samuele Gregorio, developers of Sun-plus.
