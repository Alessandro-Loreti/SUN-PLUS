The core of the project

Sun-plus is based on a microcontroller called Arduino. The two servomotors are connected to some photosensors to detect solar light position. (See pictures on the right.)

The connection has been made by using Arduino UNO, a hardware platform made up both of a circuit board, required to create the code which reads the data from the photosensors, and of a software intended to decide when and what direction the servomotors must turn.

A complete and complex harmony between information technology and green energy.
